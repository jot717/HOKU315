# INTERACTION PRESSURE ONTOLOGY

Canonical patterns for **rule-based social pressure interpretation** (Phase1-D). Not personality types, not clinical labels.

---

## emotional_labor_trap

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | You routinely absorb another person's emotional load without reciprocal recovery time. |
| **Typical behavior** | They vent; you fix, soothe, or organize the mood; the topic never quite closes. |
| **User impact** | Social time feels like unpaid care work; you leave tired even when "nothing happened." |
| **Compatible type** | Peers who self-regulate and don't treat you as the default outlet. |
| **Dangerous type** | Crisis-by-default contacts who only show up in low battery mode. |
| **Guardian tone** | Quiet, factual: name the **exchange imbalance**, not your worth. |

---

## endless_reassurance_loop

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Reassurance is requested again after each answer; closure never arrives. |
| **Typical behavior** | "Just one more time" promises; anxiety spikes when you pause. |
| **User impact** | You feel rude if you stop; the loop eats attention. |
| **Compatible type** | People who can sit with uncertainty without outsourcing it to you. |
| **Dangerous type** | Partners/friends who treat your calm as a renewable resource. |
| **Guardian tone** | Point at the **loop shape**, suggest pacing—not "be more secure." |

---

## response_pressure

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Reply speed and read receipts become implicit tests. |
| **Typical behavior** | Fast bursts when they need you; slow or vague when you need them. |
| **User impact** | You reply to reduce tension, not because you have capacity. |
| **Compatible type** | Async-friendly rhythm; silence is not punished. |
| **Dangerous type** | "Why didn't you answer?" as a recurring theme. |
| **Guardian tone** | Normalize **latency** as boundary, not failure. |

---

## dominance_interaction

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Agenda, tone, and decisions default to the other person. |
| **Typical behavior** | Interruptions, topic hijacks, "we already decided." |
| **User impact** | You rehearse objections but swallow them to keep peace. |
| **Compatible type** | Turn-taking; your "no" does not require a trial. |
| **Dangerous type** | Charismatic controllers who label resistance as drama. |
| **Guardian tone** | Describe **one-way momentum**, not power pathology. |

---

## passive_guilt_pressure

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Guilt, sarcasm, or cold distance replace direct asks. |
| **Typical behavior** | "I guess I'm the bad guy"; jokes that aren't jokes. |
| **User impact** | More energy spent decoding than resolving. |
| **Compatible type** | Direct requests with room to decline. |
| **Dangerous type** | Martyrdom narratives that make your boundaries selfish. |
| **Guardian tone** | Name **indirect cost**, not "they're toxic." |

---

## validation_extraction

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Praise hooks effort; clarity and reciprocity stay missing. |
| **Typical behavior** | Compliment → ask → vague follow-up. |
| **User impact** | You perform to earn stability that never lands. |
| **Compatible type** | Specific appreciation without a hidden invoice. |
| **Dangerous type** | Flattery-heavy dynamics in dating or work politics. |
| **Guardian tone** | Call it **attention economics**, not neediness. |

---

## comparison_exhaustion

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Social comparison becomes the background of conversation. |
| **Typical behavior** | Milestone flex, "everyone else…", humblebrag stacks. |
| **User impact** | You shrink or overcompete; rest never arrives. |
| **Compatible type** | Interest-based talk without scoreboards. |
| **Dangerous type** | Feed-like friendships that feel like audits. |

## social_performance_fatigue

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | You must appear "on" in groups or public-facing chats. |
| **Typical behavior** | Heavy @-lists, performative check-ins, no off-role space. |
| **User impact** | Fragmented focus; exhaustion after "light" events. |
| **Compatible type** | Low-stakes hangouts without audience. |
| **Dangerous type** | Always-on group cultures. |
| **Guardian tone** | Mention **exposure density**, suggest mute windows. |

## unstable_rhythm_stress

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| **Meaning** | Hot/cold pacing keeps you in standby. |
| **Typical behavior** | Plans change; affection swings without explanation. |
| **User impact** | Hypervigilance; you can't budget energy. |
| **Compatible type** | Predictable cadence; changes are announced. |
| **Dangerous type** | Intermittent reinforcement intimacy. |
| **Guardian tone** | Focus on **predictability**, not attachment style labels. |

| Field | Content |
