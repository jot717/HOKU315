# CORE PRODUCT REALIGNMENT (v1)

**STATUS:** ACTIVE direction lock  
**TYPE:** Product realignment — documentation only (no implementation mandate in this sprint)

This document restates the **true product core** for HOKU315. It complements [`PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md`](PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md), [`FOX_ROADMAP.md`](FOX_ROADMAP.md), and [`SOCIAL_SIGNAL_ARCHITECTURE.md`](SOCIAL_SIGNAL_ARCHITECTURE.md).

---

## REAL PRODUCT

HOKU315 is:

**An AI-native SNS guardian network** that helps users:

* detect haters and hostile or draining actors
* identify dangerous interaction patterns before harm compounds
* reduce emotional exhaustion from social exposure
* filter harmful social signals (noise, pressure, manipulation)
* protect mental energy in high-volume social contexts
* build safer social environments through **interaction risk intelligence**

The core is **social interaction filtering** and **SNS-oriented signal analysis**, not a generic “AI insight” surface.

---

## NOT THE PRODUCT

HOKU315 is **not**:

* a dating app
* a compatibility or “match chemistry” product
* a personality test or typology lab
* an AI therapist or clinical psychology tool
* a mood tracker or wellness journal
* a generic AI insight / report generator as the primary value proposition

Those frames may appear in legacy copy or experiments; they are **out of scope** for the stated north star.

---

## FOX ROLE

The fox **is**:

* the **guardian identity** and recognizable product persona
* the **emotional UX bridge** (calm, protective tone without clinical coldness)
* the **safety guide** that surfaces alerts and next steps in human language
* the **world character** that makes abstract “signal intelligence” legible

The fox **is not**:

* the actual core engine of the product
* a substitute for **social graph**, **SNS ingestion**, **hater models**, and **interaction risk** systems

Persona and UX layer sit **on top of** social signal intelligence; they do not replace it.

---

## REAL VALUE (MOAT)

Differentiation and defensibility come from:

* **Social signal intelligence** — what patterns mean in context, over time
* **SNS ingestion** (when shipped) — lawful, consent-based connection to user-chosen surfaces
* **Relationship / interaction graph analysis** — who drains, pressures, or destabilizes
* **Behavioral pattern detection** — repetition, escalation, inconsistency
* **Hater identification** — sustained negativity, targeting, bad-faith engagement
* **Dangerous interaction memory** — longitudinal recall of risk, not one-off scores
* **AI-native social protection** — models and policies tuned for **social harm reduction**, not generic chat
