# Deprecated product language

**STATUS: DEPRECATED**

The following vocabulary and framing are **deprecated** for outward product copy, new features, and new documentation. Existing code identifiers and contracts may remain until migrated intentionally (see [`DIRECTION_MIGRATION.md`](DIRECTION_MIGRATION.md), [`PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md`](PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md)).

---

## Deprecated terms (do not introduce in new UX / docs)

* **compatibility score** — Prefer signal-safety / clarity framing; avoid dating-style scoring language in user-facing text.
* **emotional energy** — Prefer **emotional protection** / guardian-safe wording (pairing “energy” reads dating-adjacent).
* **shared energy** — Same; prefer protection, boundaries, or signal interpretation.
* **dating-oriented wording** — e.g. pairing-first CTAs, “match wall” as primary identity, hookup or romance-first journeys.
* **romantic implication wording** — e.g. couple-chemistry copy, pickup or romance-coach tone.

### Additional deprecated concepts (PRODUCT CORE REALIGNMENT v1)

Do **not** position new outward copy or specs as:

* **AI emotional analysis** (as the product category — implies generic “how you feel” lab)
* **Emotional healing product** — we reduce **exhaustion and social harm risk**, not deliver therapy
* **Standalone insight app** — insights serve **SNS / interaction protection**, not a report-for-its-own-sake SKU
* **AI psychology system** — no clinical or diagnostic framing
* **Compatibility-first UX** — compatibility is not the core story; **social protection** is
