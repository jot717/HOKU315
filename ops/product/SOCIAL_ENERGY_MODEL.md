# SOCIAL ENERGY MODEL

Core product intelligence for HOKU315: **how social interaction spends and recovers user energy**.

Not a dating score. Not personality typing. Not therapy.

---

## response debt

**Definition:** Unanswered or delayed replies accumulate implicit obligation to "catch up."

**Signals:** ghosting_sensitivity, high_response_demand archetype, fast-window peers.

**User experience:** You reply to lower tension, not because you have capacity.

**Mitigation copy:** Widen reply windows; treat silence as neutral, not rejection.

---

## emotional carry load

**Definition:** You hold another person's mood past the conversation end.

**Signals:** emotional_extractor archetype, emotional_exhaustion_risk, emotional_labor_trap.

**User experience:** Social time feels like care work, not exchange.

**Mitigation:** End threads explicitly; cap vent sessions.

---

## reassurance loops

**Definition:** Comfort requests repeat without closure.

**Signals:** reassurance_loop archetype, approval_dependency_risk.

**User experience:** Each round of soothing spawns another.

**Mitigation:** One clear answer + pause, not infinite proof.

---

## attention asymmetry

**Definition:** Your focus outflows; theirs returns conditionally.

**Signals:** attention_drain_risk, inconsistent_attention archetype.

**User experience:** Hollowed focus without a big fight.

**Mitigation:** Track initiation balance; mute high-demand channels.

---

## pacing mismatch

**Definition:** Your natural rhythm and theirs don't align on speed and depth.

**Signals:** distance vector gap, dominant_rhythm vs async-safe need.

**User experience:** You over-explain or over-reply to prevent coldness.

**Mitigation:** Name pace explicitly; async contracts.

---

## conversation recovery cost

**Definition:** Energy required to return to baseline after a thread—not just time spent.

**Signals:** conflict_amplifier, passive_aggression_risk, value_debate_burnout.

**User experience:** One heavy chat ruins the next hour.

**Mitigation:** Cool-down before re-entry; shorter messages after conflict.

---

## emotional unpredictability load

**Definition:** Background stress from not knowing the next tone or demand.

**Signals:** inconsistent_attention, emotional_unpredictability pressure pattern.

**User experience:** You brace before opening chat.

**Mitigation:** Prefer stable-low-pressure peers when depleted.
