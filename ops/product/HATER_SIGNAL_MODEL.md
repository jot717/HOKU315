# HATER SIGNAL MODEL (v1)

**STATUS:** Conceptual only — **no ML or production detection logic** in this sprint  
**PURPOSE:** Shared vocabulary for future **hater** and **dangerous interaction** modeling

This document names **pattern families** product and research can align on. It does not define thresholds, features, or training data.

---

## Pattern concepts (examples)

| Concept | Short description |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Emotional drain pattern** | One party consistently exits interactions more depleted; asymmetry of care vs extraction. |
| **Unstable response cycle** | Hot–cold alternation that keeps the target guessing and self-blaming. |
| **Manipulation loop** | Requests framed so refusal feels cruel; boundaries punished with drama or withdrawal. |
| **Attention extraction behavior** | Baiting, vague-posting toward the user, or manufactured urgency to capture focus. |
| **Fake intimacy pattern** | Rapid closeness claims without proportionate trust history; used to lower defenses. |
| **Social pressure farming** | Public performance of victimhood or outrage to recruit bystanders against the user. |
| **Repeated negativity exposure** | Chronic downer or hostile tone toward the user or their affiliations without constructive aim. |
| **Passive-aggressive behavior** | Indirect jabs, plausible deniability, “just joking” shields after harm. |
