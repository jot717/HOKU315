## Signal-first UX law

1. **Explain signals before persona.** Headlines and CTAs name *what is analyzed* (иЁҠиҷҹгҖҒдә’еӢ•йўЁйҡӘгҖҒзҜҖеҘҸ).  
2. **One fox surfaces.** At most one short fox block on the insight result; no parallel stacks of fox / guardian cards.  
3. **One flow.** HOME вҶ’ PROFILE вҶ’ QUIZ вҶ’ TARGET вҶ’ INSIGHT вҶ’ MATCH unless the user legitimately resumes mid-flow.  
4. **No duplicate onboarding.** Each page adds *new* information; avoid repeated вҖңwelcome to the worldвҖқ paragraphs.
