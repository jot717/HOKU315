## ARCHETYPES (v1 set)

### 1. Ghoster（幽靈式）

* Inconsistent replies, emotional disappearance, delayed reassurance when reassurance matters.

### 2. Attention Drainer（情緒耗能）

* Constant emotional demand, low reciprocity, crisis-loop interactions.

### 3. Passive Aggressive（被動攻擊）

* Hidden hostility, indirect pressure, emotional ambiguity.

### 4. Validation Farmer（認可收割）

* Attention extraction, low commitment, emotional bait behavior.

### 5. Manipulator（操弄壓力）

* Guilt pressure, emotional control, instability cycles.

### 6. Chaotic Socializer（混亂社交）

* Unstable energy, unpredictable reactions, high social exhaustion.

### 7. Fake Safe Person（先甜後壓）

* Early comfort, later emotional pressure, trust extraction patterns.
