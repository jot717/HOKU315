## How the product drifted

The repository and narrative over-indexed on:

* **Emotional healing** as if the product were wellness-first
* **Guardian atmosphere** and world-building as the primary deliverable
* **AI insight feeling** — long explanations, “observation room” report tone
* **Standalone analysis experience** disconnected from **SNS**, **social graph**, and **hater / dangerous interaction** outcomes

Those elements are **supporting layers**, not the core moat.

---

## Why guardian UX became too dominant

Guardian tone and fox presence are **necessary** for comprehension and trust, but when they become the *only* story, stakeholders and contributors infer:

* the product is a **mood / insight app**
* “protection” is metaphorical only, without **social signal** substance

The fox should **carry** the value proposition, not **replace** it.

---

## Why the social layer seemed to disappear

Roadmaps and copy emphasized **fox world MVP**, **emotional safe world**, and **session memory** without equally visible threads for:

* SNS-backed ingestion
* hater and manipulation-shaped signals
* interaction graph and repeat-offender memory

Result: **social interaction filtering** read as optional future flavor instead of **core product**.

---

## Why “AI analysis” framing was incorrect

“Analysis app” positioning attracts:

* expectations of **psychological judgment** and scores
* comparison to generic LLM report products

The real product is **AI-native SNS guardian network**: filtering, detection, alerts, and **interaction risk intelligence** — with the fox as **persona + UX**, not the engine label.

---

## OLD DIRECTION vs REAL DIRECTION

| OLD (drifted) | REAL (locked v1) |
|---------------|------------------|
| Healing / safe-world as primary product | **Social protection** and **signal filtering** as primary |
| Fox world as the “what we sell” | **Fox as persona + guardian UX** on top of **SNS intelligence** |
| Standalone insight / observation report | **Interaction risk**, **hater detection**, **dangerous relationship patterns** |
| Generic emotional AI | **Hater + manipulation + drain** pattern intelligence (future-backed) |
| Compatibility / match-adjacent residue | Explicit **not dating / not compatibility core** (see realignment doc) |
